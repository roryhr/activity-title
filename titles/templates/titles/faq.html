Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Activity Title?

I like to add creative titles to my Strava activities but creativity comes in bursts, so I created this website to store activity names and update "Afternoon Run" to something else when I upload a run. This was a fun excuse build a Django app, experiment with ChatGPT for some coding help, and use the Strava API.

Where's the AI?

There's no AI here. You need to come up with your own names. All we do is store and sync them.

How does it work?

You create and save activity titles in advance. When you upload a new activity to Strava, Activity Title automatically updates it with the next one in the queue using Strava's webhook API.

Is Activity Title free?

Yes! This website is a one-trick pony that doesn't cost much to run. You can support me at paypal.me/roryhr

Strava Integration

What Strava permissions does Activity Title require?

Activity Title requires permission to read and write activity data. This is necessary to update your activity titles.

What happens to my Strava data?

Activity Title only stores your activity titles and the associated Strava activity ID. Basically, your Strava data stays on Strava. We don't change your privacy settings.

What happens if I don't authorize Activity Title to view my private activities?

Unfortunately, the app won't work. But you still log in.
