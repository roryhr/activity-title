Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Activity Title?

Activity Title is a web application that allows you to create and manage custom titles for your Strava activities. It's designed to replace generic activity names with more creative and personalized ones.

How does it work?

You create and save activity titles in advance. When you upload a new activity to Strava, Activity Title automatically updates it with your chosen title via Strava's webhook API.

Is Activity Title free?

Currently, Activity Title is a personal project and is not publicly available. I plan to open access in the future.

Strava Integration

What Strava permissions does Activity Title require?

Activity Title requires permission to read and write activity data. This is necessary to update your activity titles.

What happens to my Strava data?

Activity Title only stores your activity titles and the associated Strava activity ID. No other Strava data is stored.

Account and Access

How can I get an account?

Currently, accounts are by invitation only. If you are interested in using Activity Title, please contact me.
