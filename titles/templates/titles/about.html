About Strava Deck

Welcome to Strava Deck! This application was inspired by the famous TweetDeck, providing power users with an easy way to manage their Strava activities. It’s a personalized tool that connects directly to Strava, allowing users to schedule and manage their activities efficiently.

Our Mission

Strava Deck was built to streamline activity tracking and management for athletes and fitness enthusiasts. Our goal is to help users stay organized, set goals, and analyze their progress effortlessly using the Strava API.

Features

  • Connect directly to your Strava account to track and update activities.
  • View, schedule, and organize activities using a simple interface.
  • Personalized features for power users.
  • Manage activity titles and logs seamlessly with Strava Deck’s integrated tools.
