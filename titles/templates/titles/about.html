About Activity Title

Tired of generic activity titles like "Afternoon Run"? Activity Title lets you pre-write creative titles and effortlessly apply them to your Strava activities. Inspired by TweetDeck's scheduling features, this app helps you inject personality into your training log.

This project was a fun opportunity to build a Django app, explore the Strava API and webhooks, and even leverage the power of ChatGPT! While currently a personal project connected to my own Strava account, the goal is to expand access in the future.

How It Works

Create and save your activity titles in advance. Then, when you upload a new activity to Strava, Activity Title automatically updates it with your chosen title. No more boring default names!

Current Features

  • Create and save activity titles.
  • Automatic Strava activity updates via webhooks.
  • Easy activity title management.

Future Plans

  • Open access for other Strava users.
  • Integration with other fitness platforms (e.g., Garmin Connect).
